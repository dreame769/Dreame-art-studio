About The Artist

Kolawole Adedeji Israel

Welcome to Dreame Art Studio, where innovation meets tradition. As a pioneering Nigerian artist featured on Saatchi Art, I create one-of-a-kind artworks using two distinctive styles.

💎 Gem-Embellished Portraits: My signature technique blends traditional painting with precious gems and crystals. I embed diamonds and gemstones to create luminous facial features and stunning visual effects. Each portrait sparkles with life as light dances across the gems—creating an ever-changing masterpiece.

🌿 Nature & Environmental Art: Including works like "Nature Embrace: A Love Letter from Humanity," these pieces celebrate our connection to the environment through oil and acrylic on canvas.

Why Collect: These pieces are extremely rare. The gem technique is labor-intensive, each work takes weeks to complete, and the gems add intrinsic value. You're not just buying art—you're investing in a unique fusion of fine art and precious materials.
